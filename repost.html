Privacy Policy — RePost

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Data Collection

RePost does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All content transformation — splitting into tweets, formatting for LinkedIn, generating email drafts — happens entirely within your browser. No text you select or transform is ever sent anywhere.

Data Storage

RePost stores your custom templates, recent transforms, and preferences locally in your browser using the Chrome Storage API. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the extension.

Permissions Explained

  • activeTab — Allows the extension to read selected text from the current page when you use the context menu.
  • storage — Stores your custom templates and preferences locally on your device.
  • contextMenus — Adds "Repurpose for..." options to your right-click menu when text is selected.

Third-Party Services

If you purchase the Pro version, payments are processed by ExtensionPay and Stripe. We do not see or store your payment details. ExtensionPay's privacy policy applies to the payment transaction.

Analytics

RePost does not include any analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry. We have zero visibility into what content you transform or which platforms you target.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
