Privacy Policy — ClipBoard+

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Data Collection

ClipBoard+ does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All clipboard data is stored entirely within your browser using the Chrome Storage API. No copied content is ever sent anywhere.

Data Storage

ClipBoard+ stores your clipboard history (text, HTML snippets, and image data URLs), pinned items, and preferences locally on your device. This data never leaves your browser and is deleted when you uninstall the extension.

Permissions Explained

  • activeTab — Allows the extension to interact with the current page to paste content into editable fields.
  • storage — Stores your clipboard history and preferences locally on your device.
  • clipboardRead — Reads copied content to add it to your clipboard history.
  • clipboardWrite — Writes content to your clipboard when you select an item to paste.

Third-Party Services

If you purchase the Pro version, payments are processed by ExtensionPay and Stripe. We do not see or store your payment details. ExtensionPay's privacy policy applies to the payment transaction.

Analytics

ClipBoard+ does not include any analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry. We have zero visibility into how you use the extension or what you copy.

Changes
