Privacy Policy — TextClean

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Data Collection

TextClean does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All text processing happens entirely within your browser. No text you transform is ever sent anywhere.

Data Storage

TextClean stores only your usage preferences (favorite transforms, usage count) locally in your browser using the Chrome Storage API. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the extension.

Permissions Explained

  • contextMenus — Adds transform options to your right-click menu when text is selected.
  • activeTab — Allows the extension to interact with the current page to replace selected text.
  • storage — Stores your preferences locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

If you purchase the Pro version, payments are processed by ExtensionPay and Stripe. We do not see or store your payment details. ExtensionPay's privacy policy applies to the payment transaction.

Analytics

TextClean does not include any analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry. We have zero visibility into how you use the extension.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
