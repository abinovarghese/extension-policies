Privacy Policy — PickSelector

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Data Collection

PickSelector does not collect, store, or transmit any data to external servers. All selector generation happens entirely within your browser.

Data Storage

PickSelector stores only a local history of recently picked selectors using the Chrome Storage API. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the extension.

Page Interaction

When the inspector is active, PickSelector reads the DOM structure of the current page to generate CSS selectors and XPath expressions. It does not read, store, or transmit any page content, text, images, or user data.

Permissions Explained

  • activeTab — Allows the extension to inspect DOM elements on the current page when you activate the picker.
  • storage — Stores your selector history locally.

Third-Party Services

If you purchase the Pro version, payments are processed by ExtensionPay and Stripe. We do not see or store your payment details.

Analytics

PickSelector does not include any analytics, tracking, or telemetry.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
