Privacy Policy — DataKit

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Data Collection

DataKit does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data processing — JSON formatting, CSV conversion, Base64 encoding, JWT decoding, hashing, and more — happens entirely within your browser. No data you process is ever sent anywhere.

Data Storage

DataKit stores only your last active tab and recent inputs locally in your browser using the Chrome Storage API for convenience. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the extension.

Permissions Explained

  • storage — Stores your active tab preference and recent inputs locally on your device.

DataKit requests no other permissions. It cannot read your browsing history, access your tabs, or interact with any web page content.

Third-Party Services

If you purchase the Pro version, payments are processed by ExtensionPay and Stripe. We do not see or store your payment details. ExtensionPay's privacy policy applies to the payment transaction.

Analytics

DataKit does not include any analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry. We have zero visibility into what data you process with the extension.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
