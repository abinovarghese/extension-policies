Privacy Policy — QuickReply

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Data Collection

QuickReply does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. Your email templates and all associated data remain entirely on your device.

Data Storage

QuickReply stores your templates, categories, and usage count locally in your browser using the Chrome Storage API. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to any website you visit
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the extension

Email Content

When you insert a template, QuickReply interacts with the active text field to place the text. It does not read, store, or transmit any existing email content, recipient information, or email metadata.

Permissions Explained

  • activeTab — Allows the extension to insert template text into the compose field on the current page.
  • storage — Stores your templates and preferences locally.
  • contextMenus — Adds an "Insert Template" option to right-click menus on editable fields.

Third-Party Services

If you purchase the Pro version, payments are processed by ExtensionPay and Stripe. We do not see or store your payment details.

Analytics

QuickReply does not include any analytics, tracking, or telemetry.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
